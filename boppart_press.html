Medium Bio

Long Bio

~210 words

Michaela Boppart is the Founder of Boppart Digital — a Zürich-based AI Operating Studio she established in 2022. The studio now owns five active ventures: AIXXII, CasaFin, CHICCA, DreamNest, and LOVMINDA.

Michaela began her finance career in 2001. Two years later she co-founded Primetime Creative Group, where she ran finance, payroll, and operations for twelve years across large-scale live productions reaching audiences of up to 50'000 — concerts, corporate events, cultural productions involving international artists and global brands.

Between 2016 and 2022 she held senior finance roles at two Swiss institutions: Head of AP/AR at a Zürich-based financial services group, then Head of AP and Financial Process Optimization at a leading materials research institute in Dübendorf. Her work centered on ERP optimization, process digitalization, and end-to-end workflow automation across procure-to-pay.

Boppart Digital takes on select Platform Studio engagements and AI Consulting projects. Michaela writes regularly on LinkedIn about senior operator economics, AI in finance, and the next decade of corporate finance operations. She is available for speaking engagements across DACH in English and German.

Speaking Topics

Four talks. Each tested.

Topics Michaela speaks on — for conferences, podcasts, internal company events. Keynote (45 min) or fireside (60 min). DACH or remote.

Talk One

The Solo Operator Studio

Keynote · 30–45 minutes

Why building five products solo beats raising venture capital for the wrong reasons. The economics, the time math, the unfair advantages of disciplined burn. Includes a real-time portfolio audit of Boppart Digital.

Talk Two

AI for Senior Finance Teams

Workshop · 60–90 minutes

What corporate finance taught me about AI tool selection. Practical frameworks for evaluating AI in regulated environments. How to integrate AI without breaking SAP. Includes audit templates and tool selection matrices.

Talk Three

Building While Working

Fireside · 45–60 minutes

For senior employees considering side ventures. How to start, what to tell your boss, what to keep separate. Legal and ethical frameworks. Real numbers from the Boppart Digital journey — including how I told mine.

Talk Four

The Decision Layer Thesis

Keynote · 30 minutes

Why the next decade of consumer AI is not more tools but better routing. Drawing from the AIXXII playbook. Applicable to any fragmented market — finance, healthcare, education, legal.
