Portfolio · Five Ventures · Eight Months

What we've shipped.

Five ventures, each a different vertical, each built solo with the same disciplined stack. The proof we point to when partners ask "what does your work look like?"

01 · Portfolio Anchor

AIXXII

"The decision layer for the fragmented AI ecosystem."

Vertical
AI Operating System
Launched
June 2026
Status
Live · Growing

An AI tool catalog of 500+ curated platforms, with an intelligent routing engine (DICI) that picks the right tool for your specific goal. Solves the "47 subscriptions, only 30% used" problem at the source.

The decision layer thesis: most users don't need more tools — they need a system that picks for them.

AIXXII is the flagship venture and the proving ground for everything else in the portfolio. If we can build a decision layer for AI tools, we can build one for any fragmented market.

02 · Family Vertical

CasaFin

"Swiss Financial OS for the family that wants clarity."

Vertical
Personal Finance
Launched
Q2 2026
Status
Live · 8 Pioneers

A Swiss-built financial operating system for families. AI assistant Fina, multi-account clarity, pillar 3a planning, expense forecasting.

Built for the household that has CHF 200k+ in flow but spends weekends in Excel. Replaces the Treuhänder for the small things, supplements them for the big things.

03 · Professional Role

CHICCA

"AI that does the social media manager job."

Vertical
Social Media AI
Launching
Summer 2026
Status
Live · Founder Tier

No prompting. Just clicking. CHICCA replaces the CHF 5'000/month social media manager role — schedules, writes, repurposes, with brand voice and one-click workflows.

The thesis: most teams don't need a social media strategist. They need an executor. CHICCA is the executor — and learns their voice as it goes.

Positioned against the CHF 5k/month junior SMM, priced at CHF 19/month founder rate.
