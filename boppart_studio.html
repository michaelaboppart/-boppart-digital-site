Trust & Governance

How we handle what matters.

Six commitments. Defensible, documented, lived in every engagement. Built specifically for DACH finance teams handling sensitive numbers.

01

Swiss & EU Data Residency

All customer data stays in Switzerland or the EU. Infomaniak, Swisscom, AWS Frankfurt — never US data centres without explicit written approval.

02

No Training Data Reuse

Your data never trains anyone's AI models. Every engagement is protected by NDA from day one. AI providers configured with zero data retention where possible.

03

GDPR & Swiss DSG Compliant

Data subject requests handled within 30 days. Right to deletion respected with full audit trail. DPO contact provided on request for enterprise engagements.

04

EU AI Act Ready

Risk classification per engagement. Human-in-the-loop where required by law. Transparency documentation built into every deliverable — not an afterthought.

05

GoBD-aware (DE) · OR-aware (CH)

AI workflows in finance teams designed to preserve audit evidence and tax-compliant record-keeping. We don't introduce automation that breaks your Wirtschaftsprüfung.

06

Professional Liability Insurance

Senior advisory engagements covered. Insurance certificate available on request before contract signature. We carry the risk we ask you to take on us.

Common questions

For partners who're
doing their diligence.

Why solo?

Because every agency I've worked with had three people between me and the actual builder. Solo-operator means the person you talk to is the person who ships. Faster, leaner, more direct.

What's your capacity?

Maximum two active engagements at a time across both practices. Quality control over volume.

What if the project grows beyond solo scope?

Solo is the default. For engagements beyond solo capacity I bring in hand-picked senior collaborators I've worked with before — never juniors, never anonymous offshore. You always know who's building.

How do you handle our financial data?

NDA before every engagement. Data stays in your stack — we access via API or read-only credentials, we don't extract. Where storage is required: Swiss (Infomaniak, Swisscom) or EU (AWS Frankfurt) hosting only. Never US. Full Trust & Governance details above.

Do you work with startups or only enterprises?

Both — with one filter: I work with partners who think long-term. Startup founders who treat this as a relationship, enterprise teams that don't need to socialize every decision.

Tell us what you're building.

Or what your team is stuck on. We respond when it's worth responding to.
