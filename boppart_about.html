About · Michaela Boppart · 24 Years in Finance

From finance.
To building it.

Senior Finance Operator. Serial Founder since 2003. AI Builder. Operating Boppart Digital from Zürich since 2022.

"Twenty-four years inside finance taught me one thing: the operators who understand both numbers AND systems are the ones who scale. Everyone else gets stuck in spreadsheets."

I'm Michaela. I built Boppart Digital because the line between operating finance and building the systems that operate it is where the next decade of value lives.

Five ventures in eight months proved it to me. Now we're proving it for partners.

Finance + operations, from the start.

I started in finance in 2001. Twelve of the last twenty-four years I spent as a co-founder of Primetime Creative Group — running finance, payroll, and operations for live productions reaching 50'000 people.

Concerts. Corporate events. Cultural productions. International artists. Global brands. Complex multi-vendor execution where the numbers had to be right, the schedule had to hold, and there was never time to fix it twice.

That decade is where I learned that finance and operations are the same discipline at scale.

Then, institutional finance.

2016 – 2022. Six years inside two Swiss institutions — first a financial services group in Zürich, then a materials research institute in Dübendorf. Head of AP / AR roles. Process digitalization. ERP optimization. Workflow automation.

Multi-entity consolidation. Intercompany reconciliation. FX revaluation. Dunning processes. The full unglamorous core of how mid-cap and large organizations close books at scale.

The system that pays for itself in six months is more valuable than the system everyone is talking about.

This is where the operating muscle for Boppart Digital was built.

Now, building it.

2022 — founded Boppart Digital. Senior finance leadership for Swiss SMEs, international groups, global consumer brands. Multi-entity, multi-jurisdiction work where senior judgement is non-negotiable.

2025 — founded AIXXII. AI Operating System for decision intelligence, learning, responsible AI adoption. Now one of five ventures inside the studio.

Today the studio takes on select Platform Studio builds and AI Consulting engagements. Operating from Zürich. Available remote-first with quarterly in-person touchpoints when it matters.

If you're building something that needs both — finance precision and AI fluency — let's talk.

Operating Principles

Three convictions we work from.

I.

Precision is a posture.

Finance trained us to expect that things tie. We bring that posture to every build. The numbers reconcile. The code ships. The roadmap holds.

II.

Few things, deep work.

We say no to most opportunities. The ones we say yes to get our full attention. Solo-operator execution means the founder is also the builder.

III.

You own what you build.

No template subscriptions that expire. No vendor lock-in. We hand over code, infrastructure, documentation. The work outlives the engagement.
