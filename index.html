Boppart Digital is a Zürich-based AI Operating Studio founded in 2022. We own five ventures, build platforms for select partners, and run interim CFO engagements for Swiss SMEs and international groups.

Twenty-four years operating finance. Twelve as a co-founder. Now we build the systems that operate finance — and we don't sell tools, we build the platforms that ship them.

Our work is shaped by what senior finance taught us: precision is non-negotiable, distribution beats novelty, and the right system pays for itself within months.
